# Safety and Copyright Policy

## User responsibility

BlockThread is a tool, not a content publisher. Users are **solely responsible** for:

1. Uploading only photos of clothing they own or have explicit permission to reproduce.
2. Not uploading images that contain logos, trademarks, or copyrighted graphics they do not own.
3. Complying with Roblox's Community Standards and Terms of Service when uploading generated templates.

## Platform warnings

The BlockThread UI displays the following warnings at upload time:

> ⚠️ Only upload photos of clothing you own or have explicit permission to use.
> Do not upload images containing logos, brand marks, or copyrighted graphics.
> You are responsible for any content you upload or publish to Roblox.

## What BlockThread does NOT do

- BlockThread does not reproduce or distribute any licensed or branded clothing assets.
- BlockThread does not scrape or reverse-engineer Roblox assets.
- BlockThread does not automatically publish to the Roblox marketplace.
- BlockThread does not claim to produce output that will pass Roblox moderation.

## Logo and trademark detection (future)

A placeholder validation hook (`detectLogoOrText`) exists in `packages/core` for future integration with a logo/text detection model. This is not yet implemented.

## Roblox trademark

The word "Roblox" is used in a purely descriptive and technical context (e.g. "Roblox classic clothing template") and does not imply affiliation with or endorsement by Roblox Corporation.

If you believe a user has uploaded content that infringes your rights, please open an issue in this repository.
